The Devil May Cry games are well known for their high skill ceiling with the potential for incredibly stylish combat, using difficult techniques. However, if you played through the games you probably wouldn't know these techniques exist, and the combo videos which show off their skills in the DMC games don't usually teach these techniques. If you want to learn about advanced play in DMC games you need to actively search for it from numerous different sites, some of which are forums which haven't seen any major activity in almost a decade in the cases of DMC3 and DMC4.

Since the resources are so scattered, I've made this site to collect tech for the DMC games that I find to put them all in one place for people who are looking to improve at DMC3, DMC4 and DMC5. The site has information about all of the weapons, how to use them, and some bread and butter moves to spice up your combos for beginners, as well as information about advanced tech for more intermediate players who have a good grasp on the series' mechanics already and can benefit from learning the more obscure techniques.

About Me

In my case I consider myself an intermediate/semi-advanced player but far from an expert like Donguri990.  I have roughly 150 hours in DMC3, not many in DMC4, and around 350 hours in DMC5, so this website is also a way for me to learn some of the very obscure tech and do more research into the series that I love while hopefully helping others improve. By the time I finish this website I'm hoping to be much better at these games then when I started (as well as web design which I've never done before as you've probably noticed). Any worthwhile combos I've made will be uploaded to my channel if you're interested in checking them out.

This website will keep expanding as I find out new information, so if anybody reading this knows any techniques in any of the games don't hesitate to contact me and I'll be able to respond fairly quickly and add your information to the website. You can also send me combos you've done if you want to have them featured on the home page of the website if I get to making that into a feature.
