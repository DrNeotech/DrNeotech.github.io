Enemy DT Stun

General

In Dante Must Die difficulty on DMC3, the enemy's Devil Trigger functions differently to the other games. There are two types of attack effects on enemies, stun and knockaway. Stun is usually inflicted by all (melee) attacks, and will cause the enemy's posture to break on difficulties lower than DMD. Knockaway on the other hand is only inflicted by some attacks, Stinger will knock an enemy back, Hammer will knock an enemy down, and Hightime will launch an enemy etc.

On Dante Must Die however, enemies gain five times their usual resistances to stun and knockaway. When knockaway resistance is broken the enemy can be launched from either the air or ground, and when stun resistance is broken the enemy can be launched as long as they have not touched the ground.
