Beowulf

General

Beowulf has by far the most damage potential in Dante's arsenal, being DMC3's take on the gauntlet weapon present in most DMC games. It uses charged attacks to dish out heavy damage which are very good at breaking an DT'd enemy's posture in Dante Must Die, at the cost of very short range and area of effect for most attacks. While in DT with Beowulf equipped, Dante gains a huge buff to his defence.

Some notable moves include Beowulf's launcher "Beast Uppercut" is which can flow into Rising Dragon, doing much more damage and letting Dante follow the enemy up into the air, although this move can sometimes place Dante too far above or below the enemy depending on how much it's charged. On Dante Must Die, Beast Uppercut is one of the best launchers as it's best for breaking through the enemy's DT stun value (link to table of stun values and weapons). Killer Bee is an aerial attack that dives towards the enemy diagonally, and can be Jump Cancelled consistently once you get the hang of the timing, making it a very useful way of keeping an enemy in the air, so it's worthwhile practicing that. Beowulf's aerial Swordmaster move Hammer is also a powerful move which knocks the enemy to the floor quickly, dealing lots of damage. On tougher enemies this move can be jump cancelled and repeated, doing lots of damage and being able to break the enemy's DT stun resistance easily.
