DMC3 | Spiral

General

Spiral is a rifle which shoots high damage bullets at a slow firerate. It is fairly limited in it's uses with only 3 moves, one of which having a very long charge time, but it is still good to be familiar with it. Spiral is a long range weapon which is useful for staggering enemies, and is able to penetrate through to hit enemies behind the target. However it cannot be used in mid-air and Dante cannot move while shooting it, and the bullets travel at a fairly slow speed.

In Gunslinger, the action "Trick Shot" is the most useful ability it has, where bullets can ricochet to hit multiple enemies. When this is used on an airborne target it can hit the same enemy enough to completely stall it in the air (rephrase), which can be paired with an attack that would normally need a bit of time to charge, although because Spiral causes Dante to stand still with a long recovery animation there isn't too much time to benefit from this ability (double check). The other Gunslinger ability, Sniper, has a very long charge time and shoots a high damage bolt at the enemy, so it is not as useful for combos.
