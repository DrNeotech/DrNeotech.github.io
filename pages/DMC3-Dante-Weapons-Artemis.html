DMC3 | Artemis

General

Artemis is one of DMC's weirder ranged weapons and it can sometimes be hard to understand. It's a demonic gun with a long cooldown which shoots purple orbs that home in onto a target, and it can be charged with Gunslinger to release multiple projectiles at once, at the cost of having a slight delay after the button is released. It can be charged during other attacks, meaning it can be used well with other melee attacks to stall the enemy in the air, as well as allowing for setups that otherwise aren't possible using the burst effect and the delay.

In Gunslinger style, Artemis can use the ability Sphere, where Artemis fires* a plasma orb which is held on the end of the gun for a few seconds, which is useful for keeping a falling enemy from touching the ground. Sphere also has a Crazy Combo move, Acid Rain, where several projectiles fall from the sky, which can last for another few seconds if the style meter is at SSS.
