DMC3 | Cerberus

General

The Cerberus devil arm is a great addition to Dante's arsenal of weapons, with lots of moves that can be utilised in your combos. Cerberus is a set of nunchucks which attack very fast multiple times and can stagger enemies easily, as well as being useful for combos in the air where the multi-hit attacks keep the enemy in the air. It does little damage meaning you can get lots of attacks in without draining an enemy's health too fast. Due to how quick the attacks come out, Cerberus becomes a formidable tool when combined with Jump Cancelling.

Fast air attacks like Swing and Flicker can be jump cancelled out of for some very quick damage and air time without knocking the enemy away allowing for a follow up attack. Outside from those two Cerberus is a weapon that is best used on the ground, with it's basic attacks being useful for keeping an enemy slightly off the ground while Dante is grounded. Attacks like Crystal are also very stylish ways to launch the enemy to begin a combo, but it may be difficult to pull off as Dante does not follow the enemy into the air like with High Time on Rebellion, and it's exclusive to Swordmaster so you can't instantly teleport to the enemy either with Trickster. While in DT, Cerberus gives Dante a massive speed boost to his movement and attack speed.
