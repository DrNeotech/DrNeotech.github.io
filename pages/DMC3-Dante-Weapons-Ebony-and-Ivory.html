DMC3 | Ebony and Ivory

General

Ebony and Ivory are Dante's iconic handguns present in every Devil May Cry game, and with their simple and consistent moveset you'll probably be familiar with them. Ebony and Ivory are dual semi-automatic pistols which shoot quickly and are able to stall enemies in air with their basic shots. Like lots of the other firearms Dante has, Ebony and Ivory are very good at extending combos, but have a limited moveset. In Gunslinger it's main notable move is Rainstorm, where Dante spins in the air and shoots his guns in a sort of cone which gradually becomes more accurate. Gunslinger also lets Dante charge his weapons to deal more damage for a short period of time.
