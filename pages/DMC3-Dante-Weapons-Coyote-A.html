DMC3 | Coyote-A

General

Coyote-A is the shotgun weapon in DMC3. Like with Ebony and Ivory it's moveset stays consistent throughout the series. It is a useful way to create distance between you and the enemy, which can be useful in setting up an attack which needs distance, like Killer Bee. In Gunslinger style, the shotgun gains several more moves. These include Fireworks, where Dante spins his guns around like nunchucks, hitting nearby enemies which can be used either on the ground or mid-air, although note that it only really lands shots if the enemy is below around Dante's eye level, so if the enemy is a bit too high above or below you it won't land. Another notable move is Gun Stinger, where Dante charges towards the enemy and shoots them with the shotgun, launching them back. Like Ebony and Ivory, in Gunslinger Coyote-A can also be charged to do more damage for a limited time.
