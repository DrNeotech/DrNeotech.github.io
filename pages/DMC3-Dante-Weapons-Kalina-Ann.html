DMC3 | Kalina Ann

General

Kalina Ann is Lady's bazooka weapon which Dante gets near the end of the game. It deals a lot of damage with a decent recovery time, similar to Spiral, but with an area of effect from it's explosion, although it explodes after travelling a few meters so it has much less range.

In Gunslinger style, it gets two more moves. Hysteric fires a barrage of missiles around himself which detonate shortly after, damaging enemies around him. The move Grapple launches a hook at the enemy, which impales the enemy and can pull them towards you (this could be useful in combos so experiment with it a bit).
