DMC3 | Agni and Rudra

General

Agni and Rudra are some of Dante's strongest weapons, and are very useful for combos, especially when combined with attacks from other weapons. They're less precise than weapons like Rebellion but it's moveset is one of the strongest in the game. This is because they have many moves that can attack the enemy regardless of where they are in relation to Dante.

Agni and Rudra's aerial moves make it incredibly useful for combos against airborne enemies, with Aerial Cross which deals high damage to enemies and knocks them away, as well as the Swordmaster move Sky Dance, which is a four-hit combo that drops down to the ground afterwards, and is very useful for juggling enemies if it can be jump cancelled. Combined with their launcher which can make Dante follow the enemy into the air as well as their Air Hike ability it is one of the most useful weapons for aerial combos. Agni and Rudra is also a powerful weapon while grounded, with the Swordmaster style greatly expanding it's utility. The move Crawler can target grounded enemies from far away, and the move Twister and it's crazy combo Tempest can attack enemies directly above Dante with a wide radius. These attacks mean the weapon can be used regardless of if the enemy is above, below or far away from Dante.
