DMC3 | Nevan

General

Nevan is by far the most unique weapon in Dante's arsenal of devil arms. It's a complex weapon in the style of an electric guitar which you need a good understanding of how it works before you can properly utilise it. On top of some regular physical attacks, Nevan has several projectile playing attacks, which shoot electricity and bats at the enemy. Holding down the button plays a riff which causes the number of projectiles to increase until the button is released and the projectiles fly to the nearest opponent, before often ending with a shockwave at the end of a riff.

Along with projectile attacks, on Swordmaster it also has physical attacks. These include a basic swing attack which can be used on and off the ground, as well as a fast multi-hit attack, and an Air Raid which lets you shoot lightning bolts while flying in DT, like Alastor in DMC1. Nevan has two launcher attacks. The first being Bat Rift, which launches the enemy airborne and can follow into summoning bats above your head by holding the attack button, capable of keeping the enemy in the air. The second being Reverb Shock, which charges towards the enemy quickly, summoning projectiles nearly instantly with a range too, although it's a bit hard to follow up on. If you're able to master this weapon it's a great addition to Dante's moveset. While Dante is in DT, Nevan gives a buff to the passive Devil Trigger regeneration.
